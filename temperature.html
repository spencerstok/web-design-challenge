Cloud Latitude vs. Temperatures

This chart displays the comparison between city latitude and max temperature. It forms an interesting curve where as latitude moves away from 0* the further max temperature either increases or decreases.

While this is interesting to see visually it is not a suprise that the max high temperature tends to fall as we move further away from the equator.

It is interesting to note that there are some large spikes in temp for latiudes bewteen -40 and -20.
